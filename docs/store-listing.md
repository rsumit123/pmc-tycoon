## Full description
CHAKRAVYUH — Air Defense Grand Strategy

You are India's Head of Defense Integration. From 2026 to 2036, across 40 quarterly turns, you decide how the Indian Air Force is built, armed, and fought.

Out-procure, out-research, and out-fly three real adversaries — PLAAF, PAF, and PLAN — as their forces evolve year by year.

◆ COMMAND THE BUILD
• Allocate a realistic defense budget across procurement, R&D, readiness, and infrastructure
• Sign acquisition deals for real platforms — Rafale, Tejas, Su-30 MKI, AMCA, and more
• Run multi-year R&D programs that unlock next-gen fighters, missiles, air-defense systems, and ISR drones

◆ FIGHT THE FIGHT
• Respond to procedurally generated air-combat vignettes against named adversary forces
• Plan force packages — squadrons, AWACS, tankers, SEAD — and set the rules of engagement
• Read AI-written after-action reports that explain exactly why you won or lost
• Launch offensive strike packages and layered air defense (the chakravyuh)

◆ THINK LIKE A PLANNER
• Fog-of-war intelligence with HUMINT / SIGINT / IMINT sources of varying reliability
• ISR drone reconnaissance of adversary airbases
• Diplomacy, deliveries, force readiness, and a live theater map of the subcontinent

Semi-realistic, information-dense, and built for defense enthusiasts. No twitch reflexes — just decisions that compound across a decade.

Hold the formation.
