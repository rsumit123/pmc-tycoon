## Campaign Starting Conditions (2026-Q2, Turn 0)

### Backstory Frame
India's 2026 IAF is one year out of a **May 2025 limited air engagement with Pakistan** (real reference — "Operation Sindoor" / India-Pak confrontation following terrorist attack). IAF learned real combat lessons, lost at least one Rafale in action, and emerged with a political mandate to accelerate modernization. Campaign opens on that political momentum.

### IAF Starting State
- **31 squadrons active** against authorized strength of 42 (parliamentary panel finding Dec 2024).
- Composition: Su-30 MKI majority, Mirage-2000UPG, MiG-29UPG, MiG-21 Bison (retiring), Jaguar DARIN-III strike, Tejas Mk1/Mk1A trickling in, Rafale (2 squadrons at Ambala + Hasimara).
- Known shortfalls: pilot training pipeline strained, spares availability marginal for Russian platforms post-2022.

## Adversary Starting State (2026-Q2)

### PLAAF (People's Liberation Army Air Force)
- **J-20 inventory ~500 airframes** (grown from 300 mid-2025, trajectory toward ~1000 by 2030).
- Deployed across all five theater commands.
- **Western Theater Command** (India-facing): Hotan, Kashgar, Shigatse, Lhasa-Gonggar. J-20 brigades rotating through.
- **J-20S** (twin-seat loyal-wingman controller variant) operational.
- **J-35A** entered PLAAF service. Early induction wave.
- **J-36** (Chengdu 6th-gen demonstrator) flying as prototype with J-20S chase planes.
- Legacy: J-11B, J-10C, J-16 (strike), H-6K/J (standoff bomber), KJ-500 AEW, Y-20 transport.
- **PL-15** (200-300km BVR, Mach 5+ dual-pulse) widely deployed.
- **PL-17** (300-500km VLRAAM, AESA seeker, AWACS-killer) in service, limited numbers.
- **YJ-21** ASBM operational (carrier-killer, air-launched variant from H-6N).

### PAF (Pakistan Air Force)
- **J-10CE fleet ~36 airframes** (20 delivered mid-2025, final 16 slated 2026 with J-35-compatible data architecture for Cooperative Engagement Capability).
- **J-35E deal signed Jan 2026**: 40 airframes + option for 30 more. First 4-12 deliveries early 2026, tranche complete early 2027. **This is a Turn-0 visible event.**
- JF-17 Block 3 as lower-mid tier.
- Legacy F-16 Block 52+, Mirage-III/V (retiring).
- Doctrine note: explicit "high-low" tiering, PL-15 widely integrated.

### PLAN (PLA Navy)
- 3 aircraft carriers: Liaoning, Shandong, Fujian. Type-004 in construction.
- Type-055 destroyers (Renhai class) — YJ-21 capable.
- Type-093B / Type-095 SSN.
- H-6N (air-launched ballistic/hypersonic platform).
- Pressure axis: IOR deployments, Djibouti base, carrier group transits through Sunda/Lombok.
